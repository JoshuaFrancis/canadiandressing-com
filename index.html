In the cellar

Twelve dressings. Three regions. One pantry.

Every bottle is small batch, made in Quebec from Canadian-grown ingredients, then aged for at least eight weeks before shipping. No gums, no stabilisers, no shortcuts. Free seasonal recipe booklet with every order.

  • 01

    Maple & Black Pepper

    Single-origin Quebec maple, 12 month aged Ontario cider vinegar, cracked black pepper. Built for autumn squash, roast carrots, and any leaf with a bit of bite.

  • 02

    Saskatoon & Sage

    Prairie saskatoon berries reduced with white balsamic and wild sage from the Niagara escarpment. For grain bowls, charred lamb, soft cheeses.

  • 03

    Wild Cedar & Lemon

    Coastal BC cedar oil, Meyer lemon, cold-pressed canola. Bright, resinous, bracing. Cures a tired green salad in three minutes.

  • 04

    Smoked Honey & Mustard

    Manitoba honey smoked over apple wood, Quebec mustard seed, sherry vinegar. The all-purpose. Try it on a steak sandwich.

  • 05

    Buttermilk & Pickled Ramps

    Cultured buttermilk from a Mennonite dairy, ramps foraged in southern Ontario, Acadian sea salt. Creamy, sour, faintly wild.

  • 06

    Sea Buckthorn & Sumac

    Sea buckthorn from a Saskatchewan grower, ground sumac, cold-pressed sunflower oil. Tart, citrus-forward, the colour of a December sunrise.

FAQ

Reasonable questions, plain answers.

Where is everything made? +

Every bottle is filled in a small Quebec kitchen we share with a cider house. Ingredients are sourced direct from farms in Quebec, Ontario, BC, Manitoba, and Saskatchewan. Sea salt comes from New Brunswick.

How long do the dressings keep? +

Twelve months unopened, six weeks once opened and refrigerated. The buttermilk variety is six months and three weeks respectively. Best-by dates are printed on the cap.

Do you ship outside Canada? +

Yes. The continental US ships in two to four business days. Other regions are case-by-case, contact us before ordering and we will quote the freight before you commit.

Wholesale and stockists? +

Independent grocers can request a wholesale catalogue. Minimum order is one mixed case of twelve. Margins respect the shelf.
